The Pitch

What we built and why

Answers to the buildathon submission questions.

What's the problem?
Patients with chronic conditions turn to generic AI tools (ChatGPT etc.) for health guidance — tools that know nothing about them and can give dangerous generic advice, while their actual health data sits scattered across clinics, apps and paper.
Who has this problem?
Patients managing one or more chronic conditions (hypertension, diabetes, PCOS, anxiety, osteoarthritis) who have ongoing medication, regular check-ups, and family members to track — and who currently use Google or ChatGPT to make sense of it all.
What's the solution?
A personalised health companion integrated into Doctoranytime that reads the patient's actual records — appointments, medications, test results, family members, health metrics — before answering every question, booking every appointment, and generating every insight.
How does this serve the Companion vision?
It activates all four pillars simultaneously: it stores the medical folder, visualises the health timeline, unifies chat + visits + follow-ups into one flow, and personalises every interaction using the patient's data. Every feature feeds back into the next, compounding personalisation over time.
4/4

All four dominant use cases addressed

The app covers every DOMINANT use case from the framework and correctly refuses the ANTI case (replacing the doctor). Personalization runs as a cross-cutting thread through all features.

Which pillar each feature lives in

Your data, in one place

  • Medications with AI explanations
  • Test results with structured values & badges
  • Document vault with AI summaries
  • Family member profiles & clinical data
  • Health metrics (BP, glucose, weight, sleep)

📅 Timeline

Your story, over time

  • Doctor notes, diagnosis, prescriptions per visit
  • Monthly bar chart of visits
  • 120 days of health metric charts
  • AI sessions sidebar with conversation history

🔗 Care, Unified

Chats, visits, follow-ups

  • Book appointments from inside AI chat
  • AI popup on every page (no context switching)
  • Pre-appointment briefing 24h before visit
  • Doctor search with slide-in booking drawer
  • Medication reminders & appointment alerts
  • Pre-filled booking from chat data

✨ Personalization

Everything reflects you

  • Health memory — AI recalls past sessions
  • System prompt built from patient's real data
  • AI insights generated from actual abnormal values
  • Communication style preference (plain / technical)
  • Family context switching in AI
  • Drug interaction checks against active meds
✦ Every AI response is generated with the patient's full context injected — conditions, allergies, active medications, upcoming appointments, health summary from past sessions, and current family member context. This is what makes it fundamentally different from ChatGPT.
Four Use Cases — One Anti-Case

Where we go deep. And where we say no.

Each dominant use case is addressed by specific features. The anti-case is explicitly enforced.

1
DOMINANT

Make sense of what's happening right now

Patients receive a test result, a new diagnosis, or a prescription and have no idea what it means. They need immediate, contextual explanation — not a generic Google result.

🔬 AI analysis of test result values 💊 "Why am I taking this?" per medication 🤖 Medical Assistant with full health context 🧠 AI popup on every page — zero friction 📄 Document vault with AI summaries 📊 Status badges: normal / high / low per value
2
DOMINANT

Stay on track with care between visits

Between appointments patients forget what was said, miss follow-ups, and lose continuity. The companion bridges the gap.

📅 Pre-appointment briefing 24h before visit 📝 Doctor notes & prescriptions per appointment ⏰ Medication & appointment reminders 🧠 AI health memory across sessions 💬 "Prepare questions for my doctor" one-tap 📋 AI session sidebar — revisit past conversations
3
DOMINANT

Keep my (and my family's) health in one place

Managing health for a family with different conditions across different doctors means juggling papers, apps and memory. One unified record changes this.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family Hub — full profiles per member 🔄 AI context switch per family member 💊 Per-member medications & conditions 📁 Shared document vault 📊 Health metrics per patient 🏥 All appointment history in one timeline
4
DOMINANT

Daily life, not just doctor visits

Health happens every day — medication timing, symptom questions, metric tracking, small decisions. The companion is present in daily life, not just at appointment time.

💬 AI popup accessible from every page 📈 Daily health metric logging & charts 💡 Proactive AI insights on dashboard ⏰ Daily medication reminders 🔍 Instant doctor search with booking 🧠 "Ask AI" on any medication or test
✕
ANTI

Replace the doctor

The platform is explicitly designed NOT to replace clinical judgment. The AI answers health questions using patient data and doctor-sourced Q&A — but every path leads back to a real doctor.

Confidence scores on every AI response Sources from real doctor Q&A threads Booking CTA whenever diagnosis is mentioned System prompt: "never replace a doctor"
✦ Every AI answer ends with a path to a real Doctoranytime doctor.
